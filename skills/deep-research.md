# Skill: Deep Research

Use this skill when:
- investigating a bug
- understanding architecture
- evaluating a major change
- comparing alternatives

## Workflow
1. break the problem into smaller questions
2. form hypotheses before broad searching
3. gather evidence from code, docs, logs, and execution paths
4. validate or reject hypotheses
5. synthesize a conclusion

## Rules
- do not wander without a question
- keep scope tight
- prefer evidence over intuition
- distinguish facts, inferences, and open questions
